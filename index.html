Mission Statement:

I'm seeking a career change, I've worked in medicine, retail, film, sales and in an office setting. Now I would like to explore technology and the internet. I've learned how to code and really fell in love with the power of the internet and technology and now I feel the need to be apart of it. Although I don't have many years of experience working in this industry, I've been a technology nerd all my life, ever since I was able to play on my cousins Apple II computer. Since then I've been attached to the screen and I'm all about tech.
